Nap settling method
Why CIO doesn't work for naps: Nap sleep pressure is weaker than night. Vaughan will scream indefinitely. Use structured check-in instead — you're present, but not rocking.
😴 The nap method
  • 1
    Cot, duck & bunny in. Say "Sleep time, I love you." Leave.
  • 2
    Wait 5 minutes. The pauses in his crying are settling attempts — going in during a pause resets him.
  • 3
    If still crying: go in, pat only, 2 mins, leave. Keep it boring.
  • 4
    Wait 7 minutes → check-in. Wait 10 minutes → check-in.
  • 5
    After 30 mins total with no sleep — rescue the nap. Rock to drowsy, put down. Not failure. The cap.
  • 6
    Early wake? Hit the "woken early" button on the timer. App guides you through what to do.
His superpower: Vaughan sucks fingers, grabs duck, rolls around. He's already trying to self-settle. He just needs the runway.
One rocked nap ≠ failure. Three in a row undoes the week.
Bedtime settling
CIO works for nights. We're tightening the structure so it's not open-ended.
🌙 Bedtime — the structure
  • 1
    Routine: nappy, sleep sack, milk (lights on, keep awake), 2 songs, "sleep time", duck & bunny. Put down drowsy but awake.
  • 2
    Leave room. Hard cap: 30 minutes. Biggest change from what you've been doing.
  • 3
    30 mins: brief check-in (2 mins, pat only). Then another 15 minutes.
  • 4
    45 mins: pick up to calm only — not to sleep — back down drowsy.
  • 5
    Transfer fix: stop rocking to fully asleep. Rock to heavy-eyed, put down while still slightly conscious.
Week 1: 20–40 mins. Week 2: 10–20 mins. Week 3: 5–10 min grizzle. Corner turns around night 4–6.
Rule 1 — 5am: No parental bed after 5am. Resettle in cot. If won't settle, 6am is an acceptable start.
Rule 2 — No ladder: Pat → rock → feed rewards persistence. Pick one method per wake-up. No escalation.
Rule 3 — White noise in the room (not hallway). Continuous, moderate volume. Reduces startle wakes.
Rule 4 — Cap nap 2 at 3pm. Sleep after 3pm eats into night pressure. Grumpy afternoon > 9pm bedtime battle.
Rule 5 — Consistency beats perfection. One bad night doesn't break it. Three inconsistent days does.
Rule 6 — Bernadette's sanity matters. One free pass night per week is fine. Sleep training with a depleted parent doesn't work.

💡 Why nights are different

Night sleep is driven by circadian rhythm and sleep debt — Vaughan's body is chemically primed for sleep by 7pm in a way it simply isn't at nap time. This is why CIO works at night but fails for naps. The biology is doing most of the work. Your job is not to interfere with it.
